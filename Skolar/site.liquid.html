about skolar

Skolar’s letterforms follow conventional proportions for comfortable reading, while its stylistic details introduce warmth and character. SUCH PAGE, MUCH GIT, VERY EDIT, STATIC DYNAMIC, OH WOW

At the same time, features such as its relatively large x-height, languages, polytonic Greek, 44+ Cyrillic languages, various Latin transliterations (Pinyin, Sanskrit), Devanagari (Sanskrit, Hindi, Marathi, Nepali, …), and Gujarati.

highlights

Low-contrast and tall x-height allow for good reproduction in small sizes
Great care was given to Central and East European languages and their accents
Skolar PE supports polytonic Greek (with small caps) and automated mark placement
Devanagari and Gujarati include adaptive matras and extensive conjuncts
